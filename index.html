Privacy Policy

Trade Journal Pro is designed to respect your privacy and give you full control over your data.

1. Data Storage

All trading records, journal entries, and imported MT4/MT5 data are stored locally on your device by default. This data is not transmitted externally unless you choose to use specific features.

2. Community Features

If you choose to use the community feed or share verified trades, selected trade data may be transmitted to our server to be displayed to other users. Only the data necessary for this feature is shared.

3. No Ads or Tracking

The app does not use advertising services, third-party tracking, or analytics tools. Your data is not sold or shared with advertisers.

4. Data Responsibility

You are responsible for your own data. Uninstalling the app may permanently delete locally stored data.

5. Security

6. Third-Party Services

The app may use backend services to support features such as the community feed. These services process only the minimal data required for functionality.
